Ghana Communication Technology University (GCTU)

About Us

Ghana Communication Technology University (GCTU) is a technology-oriented institution of higher learning, committed to providing an educational experience of the highest quality. Established in November 2005, GCTU gained full public university status on 13th August 2020.

With a student population of around 10,000 and about 500 staff, GCTU fosters a vibrant research culture through multiple institutes like the Institute of Research, Innovation, and Development (IRID), the Centre for Cyber Security Research and Innovation (CCSRI), and more.

The university partners with international institutions like Coventry University (UK), Anhalt University (Germany), and Ramaiah University (India), promoting transnational education for a global edge.

GCTU offers Certificate, Diploma, Degree, and Postgraduate programs under the Faculty of Engineering, Faculty of Computing and Information Systems, GCTU Business School, and the Graduate School.

Support services like counseling, career guidance, and student clubs ensure holistic student development, preparing them for impactful careers in ICT and beyond.
